Wilkommen

This is my personal blog where I'll be talking about modifications and hacks I discover. It'll probably be:

  • FreeBSD
  • macOS/OS X
  • LaTeX
  • R
Other things may come along, just depends how I'm feeling and what I'm exploring at any given time. And whether or not I can be bothered, or feel the need, to write about. I don't intend to overtly proselytise about my methods etc., rather to document and share some of my findings, mainly so I can be sure I understand them by re-iterating them.
